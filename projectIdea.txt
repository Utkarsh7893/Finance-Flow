--> College students struggle to track daily expenses, borrowed money, and savings goals, 
    which leads to poor financial decisions due to lack of visibility and planning tools. 
    Existing finance apps are either too complex or not tailored to student-specific needs.

--> Creating a Financial Application for :
    - Students (Age 18-25)
    - Fixed Income (Usually under Rs. 25,000.00/month)
      (stipend, part-time job, allowance)
    - Need a simple UI, not like difficult corporate UI's.
    - Want privacy as a guest user.

--> Tech Stacks:
    - Node.js (Backend)
    - express (Backend)
    - React+Vite (Frontend)
    - Tailwind CSS (Frontend)
    - PostGre SQL (Database)
